Updates from the lab

2026

Feb 2026

New paper on dairy cow resilience

Our paper on genetic parameters for alternative resilience phenotypes in Holstein cows has been published in JDS Communications. This work evaluates novel indicators of dairy cow resilience based on daily milk weight perturbations. Read paper →

Jan 2026

Welcome Gabriel Zayas, André Zambon, and Ümit Bilginer

We are excited to welcome Gabriel Zayas as a new postdoctoral researcher (Ph.D. University of Florida), André Aguiar Zambon as a new research specialist (M.S. UW-Madison), and Ümit Bilginer as a new Ph.D. student (M.S. Akdeniz University, Türkiye). Welcome to Madison!

Dec 2025

Farewell to Bruno and Federica

Bruno Manuel D'Ambrosio Zeballos (Research Staff) and Federica Marín (Visiting Ph.D. Student from Uruguay) have completed their time with our lab. We wish them all the best in their future endeavors.

Oct 2025

Welcome Afees Abiola Ajasa

Afees Abiola Ajasa joins our lab as a postdoctoral researcher. Afees completed his Ph.D. at Nofima / NMBU in Norway, working on quantitative genetics and statistical genomics.

Sep 2025

EnrichKit published in Frontiers in Genetics

Our paper describing EnrichKit, a multi-omics tool for livestock research, has been published in Frontiers in Genetics. EnrichKit maps genomic coordinates to gene features and performs functional enrichment analysis for livestock species. Read paper →

Jul 2025

Guillermo Martinez Boggio joins UC Davis as Assistant Professor

Congratulations to Guillermo Martinez Boggio, who has accepted an Assistant Professor position at the University of California, Davis. Guillermo was a postdoc in our lab from 2023 to 2025 and will be greatly missed.

May 2025

greenfeedr R-package published

Our R-package for processing and reporting GreenFeed data has been published in JDS Communications. greenfeedr helps researchers and farmers process enteric methane and CO₂ emission data from dairy cattle. Read paper →

Mar 2025

Heat stress and epigenomics paper published

Our collaborative work with Dr. Jimena Laporta's group on the effects of pre-weaning heat stress on the liver transcriptome and DNA methylation in dairy calves has been published in the Journal of Dairy Science. Read paper →
